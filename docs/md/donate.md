# Donate

Santi is an [open-source project][github], and we rely on your help to develop the bot, pay hosting fees, maintain our website and more.
Donations go a long way in helping us keep the project alive, and we appreciate every single one of them.

## Perks

Donating to us also gives you the following benefits:

- A hoisted **Patron** role in [Santi Discord server][discord-server]
- Access to exclusive **#noticed** text and voice channels
- **3000 🥠 fortune cookies** on the public bot per dollar donated (after fees)

## Patreon

You can set up a monthly pledge on [Patreon][patreon] and support the project's growth, and also get fortune cookie rewards for every month you donate!

!!! Note
    Connect your Discord account on Patreon to receive your fortune cookies automatically

[![img][patreon-button]][patreon]

## PayPal

You can also donate to us through [PayPal][paypal] for one-time donations using the button below, or by donating to `santidiscordbot@gmail.com`.

!!! Note
    Mention your Discord username or user id in the payment note to receive fortune cookie rewards.
